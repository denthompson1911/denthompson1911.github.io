Our Story

We ARE
The Blueprint

Jay-Z dropped the album on September 11, 2001 — our senior year. The world changed that day. So did we. But the Class of 2001 had already been writing our own blueprint long before the charts.

Not only did we graduate in one of the most memorable years in history — our class literally designed the architectural blueprints for the new Renaissance High School, which opened its doors in 2005. We built the foundation that generations after us would walk on.

25 years later, Detroit's finest are coming home. Reunited. Reimagined. Redefined.
